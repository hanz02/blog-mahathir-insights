About Dr. Mahathir

Dr. Mahathir Mohamad
(Statesman, Visionary Leader, and Author)

Dr. Mahathir was born on 10 July 1925, in Alor Setar, Kedah, to a modest family. His father, Mohamad Iskandar, was a school principal, and his mother, Wan Tempawan, was a homemaker. Mahathir excelled academically and attended Sultan Abdul Hamid College before pursuing medicine at King Edward VII College of Medicine in Singapore (then part of British Malaya). Upon graduating in 1953, he worked as a government doctor, gaining the honorific "Dr." Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad is Malaysia's fourth and seventh Prime Minister. He is known for his transformative leadership, which steered Malaysia toward modernization and economic growth.
